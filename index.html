  • How to use it??

    • Lets assume you already installed it.
      There are three modes you can use:

    • "Hard mode" and "Easy mode". They both blocking popups that interfere with your reading experience. Easy mode leaves header/navigation bar on page, so u can change topic. Hard mode does not. There are plenty of websites that deserve it.

    • These modes are available for all pages on current website or for every page you visit while given mode is active. They are "this site" or "everywhere".

    • Last mode is "I JUST WANT TO READ!" - It disables all things that can interrupt you from reading. Works for current page until u reload it or leave in any way.

  • Cons and pros

    • Ultra-lightweight. Ultra-simple. Open source. Does things another tools do not.
